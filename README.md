### üìä Project Overview
Developed a comprehensive reporting system for AtliQ Hardware that integrates both **Sales Performance Analysis** and **Profit & Loss (P&L) Report**.  
The project processed over **1 million transactional records** to deliver automated, data-driven insights across multiple years (2019‚Äì2021), divisions, and markets.  
It supports leadership in evaluating sales growth, profitability trends, and target performance through dynamic, interactive Excel dashboards.

---

### ‚öôÔ∏è Process & Methodology
1. **Data Extraction & Transformation:**  
   - Utilized **Power Query** to perform **ETL (Extract, Transform, Load)** operations, automating data cleaning and integration from multiple Excel and CSV sources, saving **4+ hours** of manual effort per update.  

2. **Data Modeling & KPI Creation:**  
   - Established relationships between data models in **Power Pivot** and implemented **DAX measures** for key metrics including **Net Sales**, **COGS**, **Gross Margin (GM%)**, **Growth%**, and **Target Achievement%**.  

3. **Visualization & Reporting:**  
   - Built **Pivot Table‚Äìbased dashboards** and interactive reports for sales and financial performance with slicers and filters (Year, Division, Market, Customer).  

---

### üìà Sales Analysis
- Analyzed multi-year sales performance (2019‚Äì2021) across global markets and customer segments.  
- Division-level insights revealed 200%+ YoY growth, with PC and P&A divisions driving the highest increases.  
- Customer-wise analysis identified key partners like Amazon and AtliQ Exclusive, achieving 300‚Äì400% growth.  
- Performance vs Target reports highlighted an average 8‚Äì12% gap across several markets, guiding better goal-setting and forecasting.  

---

### üí∞ P&L and Financial Analysis
- Generated market-wise P&L statements showing Net Sales, COGS, Gross Margin, and GM% across 20+ regions.  
- Created quarterly GM% reports by sub-zone (e.g., India, ANZ, NA), revealing performance fluctuations and cost efficiency patterns.  
- Implemented Power Pivot and DAX to dynamically calculate financial KPIs and improve reporting flexibility.  
- Delivered automated, scalable financial models to replace manual reporting workflows.  

---

### üí° Key Insights
- Identified consistent YoY sales growth exceeding 200%, with strong market expansion across Asia and Europe.  
- Highlighted profitability disparities between markets, with GM% ranging from 26% (Germany) to 48% (New Zealand).  
- Enabled **data-driven decision-making** by integrating sales and financial metrics into unified dashboards.  
- Improved **report accuracy and transparency** for cross-departmental performance tracking.  

---

### üöÄ Results
- Reduced reporting time by **4+ hours per update** through automation.  
- Enhanced report efficiency and flexibility via DAX-based calculations.  
- Improved visibility into sales trends, revenue growth, and profitability.
